ABOUT

iStudy MENA are an educational consultancy based in Amman, Jordan. We have 8 offices in 4 countries – Jordan, Egypt , UK and Nigeria. We are proud to be the largest agency in the Middle East and North Africa region and currently partnering with over 50 institutional partners all over the UK. Our experienced and dedicated team will help you to make your study journey to the UK successful and remarkable by offering free of cost package of full academic counselling services and visa support.
